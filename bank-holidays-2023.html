What Holidays Are Banks Closed in 2023?

Banks in the United States observe several holidays throughout the year when they are closed to the public. Here is a list of the holidays in 2023 when most banks will be closed:

New Year's Day

Date: January 1, 2023
New Year's Day is a federal holiday, and banks will be closed in observance of the start of the new year.

Martin Luther King Jr. Day

Date: January 16, 2023
Martin Luther King Jr. Day honors the civil rights leader's birthday and is a federal holiday, meaning banks will be closed.

Presidents' Day

Date: February 20, 2023
Presidents' Day celebrates the birthdays of George Washington and Abraham Lincoln, and banks will be closed.

Memorial Day

Date: May 29, 2023
Memorial Day honors those who have died in military service to the United States. Banks will be closed in observance.

Juneteenth National Independence Day

Date: June 19, 2023
Juneteenth commemorates the end of slavery in the United States, and banks will be closed in observance of this federal holiday.

Independence Day

Date: July 4, 2023
Independence Day celebrates the adoption of the Declaration of Independence, and banks will be closed.

Labor Day

Date: September 4, 2023
Labor Day honors the American labor movement and workers, and banks will be closed.

Columbus Day

Date: October 9, 2023
Columbus Day celebrates Christopher Columbus's arrival in the Americas, and banks will be closed.

Veterans Day

Date: November 11, 2023
Veterans Day honors military veterans, and banks will be closed in observance of this federal holiday.

Thanksgiving Day

Date: November 23, 2023
Thanksgiving Day is a time for giving thanks and spending time with family. Banks will be closed.

Christmas Day

Date: December 25, 2023
Christmas Day celebrates the birth of Jesus Christ, and banks will be closed in observance.


These holidays are observed by most banks in the United States, and it's important to plan your banking activities accordingly. If you have any specific needs, be sure to check with your bank for their holiday schedule.
